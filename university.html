Bachelor of Business Administration

University of Nairobi • 4 year undergraduate course
Call center : 07 100 00 000
  • Overview
  • Prospectus
  • Syllabus

This course is designed for those who would like to build a career in management, mainly in the profit-driven sectors. The programme carefully combines theoretical and practical learning as part of its training approach and incorporates work-related learning to ensure the production of graduates with both the knowledge and the skills to succeed in the cutthroat business sectors in the world.


What we offer

Students learn to connect business concepts to the larger global society, with preparation for a clear career path within the field of business including;

  • Analysis of business problems and opportunities
  • Key business functions, such as leadership and entrepreneurship
  • Analysis and implementation of business decisions and theories
  • A community designed to support and inspire you

Who is this course for?

  • Operations Manager
  • Business Process Analyst
  • Business Consultant
  • Business Lecturer
  • Branch Supervisor
  • Branch Manager
  • Country Manager
  • Change Management Consultant

Why choose University of Nairobi

The programme consists of 11 core (required) modules and 8 elective modules. The Research Methodology is a pre-requisite for the dissertation in this course. Some modules may be co-requisite (i.e. such modules are required to be taken together).

The number at the end of the module in parenthesis indicates the credit load of the module. 1 credit is equal to 10 hours of learning (guided, in class and independent combined); therefore a 10-credit module requires on average 100 hours of learning from the student. The Programme will be Block Release and Distance Learning.


Employability and graduates

Univerisity of Nairobi provides quality support services and programmes that advance student access to learning, welfare, wellness and talent development through Student and Alumni Affairs Department (SAAD).


Important points to remember when applying

All students can apply in person or through the online application form available on the website. Please read the “Steps to Apply” document available on the application portal before applying.

  • Please present clear certified copies of all relevant educational certificates.
  • Please present clear certified of Omang/passport/identity card.
  • Please read the Fee Structure and Policy document available on the website before applying.
  • Should you have any doubts on the application process, please contact our admissions department for further assistance.
